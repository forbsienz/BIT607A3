Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

When you book an appointment with Snazzy Paws Grooming, we collect basic contact information including your name, phone number, email address, and details regarding your pet. This data is essential for managing our scheduling system.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for appointment reminders, communicating about grooming services, and internal business operations. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to ensure your personal information is protected against unauthorized access. Web bookings are processed securely.
